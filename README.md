Mine AI is an AI-powered chatbot designed to assist mining professionals by providing quick, accurate, and reliable information related to mining Acts, Rules, and Regulations.

Mining laws such as the Mines Act, MMDR Act, DGMS guidelines, Coal & Metalliferous Regulations, and safety/environmental rules are usually lengthy and complex. Searching them manually is time-consuming and error-prone. Your project solves this problem by offering an intelligent conversational system that delivers relevant legal information instantly.
